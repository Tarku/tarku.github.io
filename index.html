Short presentation

Hi! I am Tark, a years old hobbyist conlanger[1], worldbuilder[2], musician, language-loving nerd, programmer, and polyglot(-ish).
This website is pretty much not very beautiful - very 1990's - as I am not good at CSS, nor is very sophisticated.
Below this though, you'll find many of my projects.

Conlanging projects

  • My semitic-inspired old conlang written in the Arabic script, Jachikh (Incomplete).
  • A conlang inspired by Hungarian and Turkish, Jedhlek.
  • Yet another semitic inspired conlang, but with more experience with conlanging and sense of aesthetic, Mowyāwan (Incomplete).
  • A complete, kitchen sink language, inspired by many languages and prolly one of the favorite conlangs I've made, Koritira.
  • A romance conlang of mine, Roumanic (Lhembà Roumanigà), the document is in French though.
  • A proto-lang and its descendants, Proto-Itelsamar, Old Itelsamar and Old Ewizmar.
  • A Finnish and Turkish-inspired conlang spoken in the vast taigas of the Shänland, Shäny.
  • Yet another proto-lang and its descendants, spoken in Chashili and on the western coast of the Shänland Proto-Yaltino-Chashilan, Chashilan and Yaltinan.
  • A fairly weird language with both vowel and consonant harmony, and spoken in the jungle, Ydzan.
  • A conlang with a relatively simple phonology, spoken on the small, fairly subtropical island of Bat Ekanát, alongside with its offspring Nellá, with an orthography inspired by Quenya's, Nédelat and Nellá.

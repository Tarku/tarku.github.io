## Jachikh

**Jachikh** (جَشِٓخ تَحًق /ˈd͡ʒa.t͡ʃix.ta.hɛq/ in Jachikh) is a conlang that I have created in 2019.
Its grammar is based on triconsonantal roots (like Arabic) and the syntax is mainly OSV.

About me

This page is where I will present myself into more details than in the homepage.

Identity

My name is Tark, I am a 19 year-old French student in linguistics.

I use he/him pronouns.

Languages spoken

I speak native French and am fluent in English. Other languages I can speak a bit of include German, Japanese and Turkish.
I also have some amount of knowledge about Latin, which is a cool language.

Hobbies

As said in the homepage, I make conlangs on a regular basis and create worlds.

I also have an interest in general linguistics, historical linguistics, craftsmanship (weaving, sewing, woodworking) and gardening.

I play the piano and I sometimes write music, even though I rarely like what I make since I have no idea of what I'm doing.

Regarding music, I listen to many different styles, even though I really like Anatolian Rock, which is a style of music that is characterized by being rock, but which includes traditional Turkish musical instruments such as the saz (also known as bağlama), and having lyrics that are predominantly sung in the Turkish language.


My favorite musicians of that style are Barış Manço, Erkin Koray and Cem Karaca.

I also listen to other musicians belonging to different genres of Turkish music, such as Fikret Kızılok and Neşet Ertaş, as well as the Dutch-Turkish band Altın Gün, whose music is pretty great.

I am also a programmer, I predominantly use C++, C#, Python and JavaScript.
